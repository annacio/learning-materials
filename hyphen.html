Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Before or After?

Choose the correct form:

  1. "The (well-trained / well trained) staff performed excellently."
  2. "The staff was (well-trained / well trained)."
  3. "We offer (state-of-the-art / state of the art) equipment."
  4. "Our equipment is (state-of-the-art / state of the art)."
Answers:
1. well-trained
2. well trained
3. state-of-the-art
4. state of the art
